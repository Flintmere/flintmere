# outreach.md — Targets and log

Cold outreach obeys UK PECR + GDPR. Every email identifies itself as outreach, states lawful basis (B2B legitimate interest), names the sender, and offers a clear opt-out. No pretend-personal messages. No mass blasts. Relationship-first.

Legal Council (#9, #23, #24) gates every outreach draft. **#24 veto** on anything touching data handling or merchant representation.

## Target categories (Flintmere-specific)

### Shopify agencies (Plus Partners + growth agencies)

- **Shopify Plus Partners directory** — screen-scraped + targeted by vertical fit (ecom-ops, AI-forward agencies).
- **Growth agencies** managing 5–50 Shopify client stores (non-Plus Partners).
- **Angle**: Agency tier pitch — 25 client seats, white-label reporting, API access. One sale = 25 stores on the platform. Strongest ROI channel.

### Individual Shopify merchants (low-scoring)

- Merchants whose scanner score is <50/100 and who opted into email capture on the scanner.
- **Angle**: personal walk-through of their top 3 fixes on a 15-minute call. No pitch — just value. Converts to Growth or Scale tier organically.

### Shopify ecosystem + Partner team

- Shopify Dev Relations (specifically people who post about agentic commerce, Catalog, Catalog Mapping).
- Shopify Plus Partner team.
- **Angle**: listing feedback, App Store submission support, potential feature co-marketing when launch credibility is established.

### Complementary tool vendors (integration prospects)

- PIM vendors with Shopify integrations (Plytix, Sales Layer, Pimcore for Shopify Plus).
- SEO apps (Schema Plus, JSON-LD for SEO, Booster SEO).
- Headless platforms (Hydrogen agencies, Composable stacks).
- **Angle**: integration proposal — Flintmere reads from PIM / feeds into SEO schema / works with headless. Non-competitive surface alignment.

### Security researchers + industry press

- Ecom-specialist journalists (Modern Retail, Retail Dive, RetailWire).
- Shopify-ecosystem publications (eComExperts, Shopify Masters podcast).
- AI-commerce researchers (academic + industry).
- **Angle**: research data from our aggregate scanner / beta merchant base. "State of AI Readiness in Beauty" as the flagship piece.

### Grant / funding programmes (later)

- Not a day-one target. Revisit at month 4+ once traction exists. Possible routes: grants for SME productivity tooling, UK / EU digital-commerce programmes.

### Shopify Partner + Plus community events

- ShopTalk (US), ShopTalk Europe, IRCE.
- Shopify Unite (when it resumes).
- Regional Shopify meetups (London, New York, Toronto).
- **Angle**: sponsorship of Agency-track sessions; speaking slot on "AI readiness" research.

## Standing rules for drafts

1. **Identify sender + purpose in the first two lines.** "I'm Abu Aaliyah from Flintmere. We make catalog readiness scoring for Shopify agencies."
2. **State lawful basis once** — "we're reaching out under B2B legitimate interest" — or link to a public outreach policy page.
3. **One specific ask.** Not a menu.
4. **Include a one-click opt-out** on every email.
5. **Reference a concrete piece of their work** — real context, not boilerplate flattery.
6. **No attachments.** Link to the scanner or the one-pager.
7. **Max 150 words** unless replying to an ongoing thread.
8. **Send time-zoned.** Send in the recipient's business hours, not ours.
9. **Bracket signature in the subject line** when appropriate — but only when it's the key noun. Don't bracket "hello".

## Example drafts (canonical templates)

### Cold-to-agency (Agency tier target)

> Subject: Flintmere — `[ scorecard ]` for your Shopify Plus clients
>
> [Name], I saw your case study on [client name]'s 40% Q3 growth — impressive work.
>
> I'm Abu from Flintmere. We score Shopify catalogs for AI-agent readiness — the data agents like ChatGPT parse when they recommend products. It's the kind of audit that sits nicely under an agency retainer: merchants see a specific score improve over time, and you have a measurable artefact for QBRs.
>
> Agency tier (£399/mo, 25 client seats, white-label) might be worth a 15-minute look. Or I can just send you a scan of one of your client stores — no pitch, just data.
>
> Either way, reply and let me know. If this isn't relevant, reply "no thanks" and you won't hear from me again.
>
> Contact: abu@flintmere.com · Opt out: [link]
>
> (We're reaching out under UK B2B legitimate interest. Flintmere Ltd, company no. XXXXXXX.)

>
> Not here to sell anything — happy to walk through your top 3 fixes on a 15-min call if useful. Most of our scanner users apply the first tier of fixes themselves and move into the 60s without paying us a penny.
>
> Reply if useful, or reply "no thanks" and you won't hear from me again.
>
> Opt out: [link]

### Ecosystem / integration-partner outreach

> Subject: Flintmere + [Vendor] — `[ integration ]` proposal
>
> [Name], I'm Abu from Flintmere. We score Shopify catalogs for AI-agent readiness.
>
> [Vendor] handles PIM beautifully for our target merchant profile (mid-market + agency). Most of our customers eventually hit the point where their PIM is their source of truth and Flintmere should be reading from it.
