# outreach.md — Targets and log

Cold outreach obeys UK PECR and GDPR. Every email identifies itself as outreach, states lawful basis (B2B legitimate interest), names the sender, and offers a clear opt-out. No pretend-personal messages. No mass blasts. Relationship-first.

Legal Council (#9, #23, #24) gates every outreach draft. #24 has veto on anything touching data handling.

## Target categories

### Wallets

- Major: MetaMask, Rabby, Rainbow, Trust, OKX, Phantom (EVM side), Safe, Zerion.
- Angle: integration of read-only approval-risk data into the wallet UI.

### DeFi protocols

- AMMs, lending, yield: Uniswap, Aave, Pendle, 1inch, Curve, Balancer.
- Angle: user-facing "approvals on this contract" insight, or treasury monitoring for their own multisig.

### L2 / chain ecosystems

- Base, Arbitrum, Optimism, Scroll, zkSync, Linea, Mantle.
- Angle: grant programmes, ecosystem listings, partner blog co-writes.

### Security researchers & press

- Independent: known wallet-security researchers who publish threads / newsletters.
- Press: The Defiant, Bankless, Decrypt, CoinDesk security desk.

### Grant programmes

- Ethereum Foundation, Optimism RPGF, Arbitrum STIP, Base Builder grants, Gitcoin rounds.

### Newsletters & communities

- DL News, Milk Road, The Daily Gwei, Week in Ethereum News.
- Security-focused Discords and DAO ops forums (by referral only).

## Standing rules for drafts

1. Identify the sender and the purpose in the first two lines.
2. State lawful basis once ("we're reaching out under B2B legitimate interest") or link to a public outreach policy.
3. One specific ask. Not a menu.
4. Include a one-click opt-out line.
5. Reference a concrete piece of their work — not boilerplate flattery.
6. No attachments. Link to the one-pager and the repo instead.
7. Max 150 words unless replying to an ongoing thread.

## Append log

Format:

```
### YYYY-MM-DD — <target name>

- Category: <wallets | protocols | l2 | research | grants | newsletters>
- Ask: <one line>
- Status: <sent | replied | meeting booked | declined | no reply — date>
```
